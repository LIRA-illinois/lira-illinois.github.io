date: 2024-12-13
---

Our paper titled ["On Generating Explanations for Reinforcement Learning Policies: An Empirical Study"](/files/tl-search.pdf) was accepted to [IEEE Control Systems Letters](https://www.ieeecss.org/publication/ieee-control-systems-letters)! We present a local-search method for explaining reinforcement learning policies using temporal logic. This work was led by Miki Yuasa.
