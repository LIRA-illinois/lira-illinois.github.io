---
title: "Our paper on pathfinder operations was accepted to ITCS 2025!"
date: 2025-07-01
---

Our paper titled ["Pathfinders in the Sky: Formal Decision-Making Models for Collaborative Air Traffic Control in Convective Weather"](/files/pathfinder.pdf) was accepted to [ITSC 2025](https://ieee-itsc.org/2025/)! We propose and analyze a model for pathfinder operations. This work was in collaboration with Dr. Max Li.
