---
title: "Our paper on ad hoc teaming was accepted to AAMAS 2026!"
date: 2025-12-02
---

Our paper titled ["Zero-Shot Coordination in Ad Hoc Teams with Generalized Policy Improvement and Difference Rewards"](/files/gpat.pdf) was accepted for publication as a full paper at [AAMAS 2026](https://cyprusconferences.org/aamas2026/)! We show that generalized policy improvement and difference rewards improve zero-shot coordination in a vareity of environments, including Overcooked. The work was led by Rupal Nigam.
